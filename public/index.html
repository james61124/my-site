About Me

Hi everyone, you can just call me James. I’m a software guy, though I’m not really a fan of hardware.

I used to find school life quite boring and felt like I was just studying too much. But after entering university, many interesting things started happening. I did things I never thought I could do before and met many different kinds of people. Recently, I’ve been feeling that I should document my life, or else all these wonderful memories might just fade away.

This is my blog, where I record my life journey and share some technical insights. For now, since I’m doing an internship in the Netherlands, I’ll mostly be writing about my travel experiences here. If you have any suggestions or topics you’d like me to cover, feel free to message me or leave a comment!
